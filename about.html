Who We Are

Where Busy People Find Easy Solutions

Welcome to FitHUb Nutrition Center, located in the heart of Supercity Bhadaj, Ahmedabad. We proudly deliver a complete wellness experience for Body, Mind & Soul.

We believe that wellness isn't just about weight loss — it's about unlocking your highest potential physically, mentally, and emotionally. Our goal is to help you live a healthier, more fulfilling life, no matter how busy your schedule may be.

Founded by Wellness Coaches Mitesh Patel and Trupti Patel, with over 10 years of professional experience in the wellness and nutrition industry, our mission has reached beyond India, impacting lives in multiple countries. We don't believe in shortcuts or "one-size-fits-all" plans — every individual is unique.
